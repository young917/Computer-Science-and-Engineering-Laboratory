#Description

	Puzzle Game
	After clicking "시작", alphabet puzzle pieces are shuffled.
	When clicking the puzzle piece adjacent to blank, It moves to position where blank piece located.
	By doing move puzzle pieces, make
	A B C D
	E F G H
	I J K L
	M N O  .

#Requirement

	Alphabet images('A' to 'O') and blank image.(total 16)
	Name alphabet images "1.gif" ~ "15.gif".
	Name blank image "16.gif".
